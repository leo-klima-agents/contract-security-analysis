Contract Access Analyser

Inspect AccessControl / AccessManager roles, Ownable ownership & Safe multisig config of a contract via the BlockScout API, then validate the reconstructed state against the live contract on-chain.

Custom role names (optional)
Known accounts (optional)
Data from BlockScout; transaction & address links open the chain's canonical explorer (Etherscan / BaseScan / PolygonScan / Arbiscan). Role holders, owners and Safe multisig config are reconstructed by replaying RoleGranted/RoleRevoked/RoleAdminChanged, OwnershipTransferred, and Safe SafeSetup/AddedOwner/RemovedOwner/ChangedThreshold events since deployment. Labels are best-effort. The Verify on-chain button cross-checks every holder, owner, module & threshold against the live contract via eth_call (hasRole / getOwners / getThreshold / isModuleEnabled / owner).
